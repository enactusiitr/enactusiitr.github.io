Who we are

Enactus IIT Roorkee is a dynamic student-led organization driven by the belief that entrepreneurship can be a powerful tool for social change. By bringing together students, academic mentors, and industry leaders, we develop innovative solutions to tackle some of the most pressing social and environmental challenges. With a global network spanning 33 countries, 1,064 campus programs, and 500 corporate partners, Enactus has impacted over 13.1 million lives worldwide.

Our Projects

At Enactus IITR, we take pride in designing and developing innovative projects that push the boundaries of technology and creativity, our projects reflect our passion for excellence. Take a look at some of our latest work and discover how we are making an impact.

Our Vision

We envision a world where entrepreneurship fuels progress, where every challenge inspires innovation, and where impact is measured not just in profits, but in lives transformed. At Enactus IIT Roorkee, we strive to empower communities by building sustainable solutions that create long-term social and economic value.

Our mission is to foster a culture of ethical entrepreneurship, where students apply their creativity and skills to solve real-world problems. By bridging the gap between business and social good, we are committed to creating a future where innovation leads to inclusive growth, sustainability, and empowerment. Through every project we undertake, we aim to drive meaningful change, proving that businesses can thrive while making the world a better place.
