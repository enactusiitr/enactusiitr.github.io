A Proud Moment for Enactus IIT Roorkee
We are thrilled to announce that Project StubXtract has been selected for the National-Level Hult Prize Competition to be held at IIT Bombay. Competing against some of the brightest young innovators across the country, Team StubXtract from IIT Roorkee will present its groundbreaking solution to tackle one of India's most pressing environmental challenges—stubble burning.

The Hult Prize, often referred to as the "Nobel Prize for Students", is a prestigious global competition that challenges university students to develop social entrepreneurship solutions for critical global issues. This year's theme revolves around sustainability and impact-driven innovation, making StubXtract a perfect fit for the challenge. With this achievement, our team is not just representing IIT Roorkee but also pioneering a sustainable future for Indian agriculture. But what exactly is StubXtract? Let's explore.

StubXtract: Turning Stubble Waste into a Goldmine


Every year, thousands of farmers in India resort to stubble burning, a practice that releases toxic pollutants into the air, worsens climate change, and depletes soil fertility. Despite government regulations, the lack of cost-effective alternatives forces farmers to continue this harmful method.

This is where StubXtract comes in. StubXtract is an innovative biorefinery-based solution that converts agricultural waste (stubble) into high-value bio-based products like vanillin, polylactic acid (PLA), and lactic acid, all of which have significant industrial applications in food, packaging, and medical fields.

How Does StubXtract Work?


StubXtract introduces a small-scale, mobile, in-situ biorefinery unit that can be deployed directly in fields, allowing farmers to process their crop waste on-site. Instead of burning, they can extract valuable chemicals from stubble and sell them for profit.

Key Technologies Behind StubXtract

  • In-Situ Biorefinery – Portable units that process stubble on farms, eliminating transport costs
  • Hemicellulose Hydrolysis – Converts stubble into xylose, a key ingredient for sugar substitutes
  • Lignin Valorization – Extracts vanillin, an eco-friendly alternative to synthetic vanilla
  • Cellulose to PLA & Lactic Acid Conversion – Produces biodegradable bioplastics for food packaging and medical use
  • Custom Hiring Centers (CHCs) – A Technology-as-a-Service model making it affordable for farmers to rent and use the technology

Why StubXtract is a Game-Changer

Environmental Impact

  • Prevents millions of tons of CO₂ emissions caused by stubble burning
  • Improves air quality, reducing respiratory diseases in cities
  • Restores soil fertility, ensuring sustainable agriculture
  • Reduces dependency on petroleum-based industrial chemicals

Economic Impact

  • Creates a new revenue stream for farmers by turning waste into valuable products
  • Affordable through a rental model (CHCs) for small-scale farmers
  • Generates employment in rural areas for equipment operation and bioprocessing

Social & Agricultural Impact

  • Improves public health by reducing pollution-related diseases
  • Boosts long-term farm productivity by preventing soil degradation
  • Establishes a circular bioeconomy, benefiting farmers, industries, and local communities

Envision, the flagship event hosted by Enactus IIT Roorkee, is an extraordinary platform designed to bring together some of the brightest minds in social entrepreneurship. This event, in collaboration with E. Summit 2025, promises to be a milestone in the realm of social change, inspiring innovative solutions to some of the world's most pressing challenges. Held from 31st January to 2nd February 2025, Envision aims to empower student-led initiatives by providing them with the tools, exposure, and mentorship needed to bring their transformative ideas to life.

A Premier Gathering of Social Entrepreneurs


Envision stands as one of the most significant gatherings of social entrepreneurs in India, uniting 450+ participants and 80+ teams from across the country. These teams represent the essence of social entrepreneurship—driven by passion, purpose, and the desire to create sustainable change. Each team at Envision brings forward groundbreaking projects, designed to solve real-world social challenges, ranging from poverty alleviation and healthcare to education and environmental sustainability.

This year, the event promises to be a landmark occasion in terms of participation and the quality of innovative solutions presented. Enactus teams from universities all over India will showcase their work, engage in meaningful discussions, and find innovative ways to scale and implement their ideas.

Key Highlights of Envision 2025


1. Encouraging Innovative Problem-Solving:

At its core, Envision is about empowering young minds to solve real-world social challenges. The event encourages students to think creatively, push boundaries, and come up with solutions that can make a tangible difference in communities. Whether it's a tech-driven solution for underserved communities or a sustainable model for rural development, Envision is all about breaking the mold and finding unique ways to address societal issues.

2. Nationwide Participation and Exchange of Ideas:

One of the standout features of Envision is its nationwide participation. With over 80 teams from diverse backgrounds and regions, the event fosters a vibrant exchange of ideas. This cross-cultural collaboration allows for a mix of perspectives, encouraging participants to think beyond borders and work collectively towards solutions that can be implemented on a national, and even global, scale.

3. A Platform for Scaling Impactful Ideas:

Envision isn't just about presenting ideas—it's about taking them to the next level. With opportunities to network with potential investors, mentors, and industry leaders, the event provides a platform to scale impactful social projects. The support provided at Envision can help these young entrepreneurs grow their initiatives and turn their vision into reality, ultimately creating a lasting impact on society.

4. Connecting Entrepreneurs with Investors and Mentors:

The event opens doors for budding entrepreneurs to connect with potential investors and mentors, who can offer invaluable guidance and funding. This collaboration is vital for the growth of social enterprises, helping them navigate the complexities of scaling their operations, securing funding, and reaching wider audiences.

5. Collaborations Between Enactus Chapters:

Envision also serves as a catalyst for collaboration among different Enactus chapters. The event helps foster connections between teams, enabling them to share knowledge, experiences, and resources. These collaborations lead to the development of joint initiatives, allowing Enactus chapters to pool their strengths and create even more impactful projects that tackle global challenges.

A Vision for the Future


Envision 2025 is more than just a competition—it is a testament to the power of young social innovators to make a real difference in the world. By encouraging collaboration, fostering innovation, and connecting student-led initiatives with essential resources, Envision plays a pivotal role in shaping the future of social entrepreneurship in India.

As we look ahead to the event, the excitement and anticipation are palpable. With more teams, more ideas, and more opportunities to drive change, Envision is set to be an unforgettable experience for everyone involved. Whether you're a participant, mentor, or investor, Envision 2025 promises to be a unique journey of growth, learning, and, most importantly, impact.

Get ready to be a part of the future of social innovation. See you at Envision 2025!
